The Basics

Dtor is a copy of the private cryptocurrenty Monero with these deviations:

  • Deflationary finite supply of 184.467.440 coins
  • Slow and steady emission over 50 years
  • 2 minute block
  • Algo: CryptoNight v7

Dtor is a fairly launched coin with no premine or ICO. Dtor is not a "fork" of another blockchain. With its own genesis block there is no degradation of privacy due to ring signatures using different participants for the same tx outputs on opposing forks.
