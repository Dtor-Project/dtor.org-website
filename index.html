The Basics

Dtor is a copy of the private cryptocurrenty Monero:

  • A capped emmision that disallows infinite coin creation
  • Total supply 18,400,000 dtor

Dtor is a fairly launched coin with no premine or ICO. Dtor not a fork of another blockchain. With its own genesis block there is no degradation of privacy due to ring signatures using different participants for the same tx outputs on opposing forks.
